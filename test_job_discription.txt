Job Title: Senior Product Designer (UX/UI)
Company: DesignTech Solutions
Location: Remote (Optional: Hybrid or On-site in New York, NY)
Job Type: Full-time

About DesignTech Solutions:
DesignTech Solutions is a fast-growing digital agency specializing in user experience, product design, and brand strategy. We work with global clients to deliver cutting-edge SaaS platforms, e-commerce websites, and mobile applications. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and human-centered design through innovative, data-driven solutions.

About the Role:
DesignTech Solutions is looking for a Senior Product Designer (UX/UI) to lead the design and development of engaging digital experiences. This role requires expertise in design systems, UX research, and interactive prototyping. If you're passionate about creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, we'd love to have you on our team!

Responsibilities:
Design and develop engaging user interfaces for SaaS platforms, e-commerce sites, and mobile applications.

Build and maintain a consistent design system across multiple products.

Conduct UX research, competitor analysis, and user interviews to improve product usability.

Create interactive prototypes for usability testing, sales presentations, and development handoff.

Work closely with developers to ensure smooth design implementation.

Oversee bug-fixing and quality assurance post-development.

Required Skills & Experience:
10+ years of experience in UX/UI design, product design, or graphic design.

Strong background in wireframing, prototyping, and user research methodologies.

Hands-on experience with Figma, Sketch, Adobe Suite, Framer, InVision, and Miro.

Understanding of HTML & CSS for design execution.

Bachelor's Degree in Industrial & Graphic Design or a related field.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience in motion graphics, animation, and branding.

Previous work on large-scale websites and international projects.

Benefits & Perks:
Competitive salary ($100,000 - $130,000 per year)

Flexible remote work options (Hybrid & On-site available)

Health, Dental, and Vision Insurance

Professional development budget for courses, conferences, and certifications

Collaborative and creative work environment
